Subscription Details

Locked Subscriptions

PUBLIC alerts are required for all devices. This issues reminders that go out to all students and is issued by The Scratch staff on behalf of administration.

CRITICAL alerts are required for all devices. These are alerts sent out by administration directly and is used for highly important news.

Optional Subscriptions

WEATHER alerts are optional. These are announcements every school day with today's forecast and any active weather alerts.

Guidance alerts are optional, but highly recommended. These are issued by your guidance counselor to remind students of important dates.
